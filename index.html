Hajj and Umrah Steps & Rituals

  1. Step 1: Ihram - Enter the state of spiritual purity.
  2. Step 2: Tawaf - Circumambulate the Kaaba seven times.
  3. Step 3: Sa'i - Walk between the hills of Safa and Marwah.
  4. Step 4: Standing at Arafat - Spend the day in prayer.
  5. Step 5: Stoning the Devil - Throw stones at the pillars.
  6. Step 6: Qurbani - Sacrifice an animal.
  7. Step 7: Tawaf al-Ifadah - Perform another circumambulation.
